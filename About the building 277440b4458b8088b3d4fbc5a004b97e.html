👉 Common Areas

The campus is located on the 4th floor. It features a study area with 40 seats, a silent area, a meeting room, a kitchen area, and sofa pods. The school is open 24/7 and is accessible with a key tag. There are entrances on both sides of the building and an elevator. Key cards are available for all kood/Võru students to access our campus. If you need a key tag, please contact Ingrid or Annika. If you wish to study at night, be respectful towards others.

  • 1st floor is the entrance.
  • 2nd floor is Pangasaal. This is used jointly by kood/Võru and Võru Linnavalitsus for special events. You can only access this area when necessary, as the hall is locked.
  • 3rd floor is for toilets and wardrobe. Please use the wardrobe for your shoes and coats.
  • The 4th floor is our campus and open for everyone to study.

👉 Good to know:

  • Please wear slippers/indoor shoes in the building for your own comfort and tidiness!
  • Students can use parking lots around and near the campus. Parking is free of charge in Võru.
  • Want to bring a friend over? Please inform the team beforehand.
  • We are pet-friendly, but you have to let others know if you bring your pet. Some people have allergies and are not fans of pets.
  • The tap water is drinkable. ✅
And if something happens (e.g. coffee spilt on the couch, monitor broken, or something else) then let the team know ASAP. Overall keep tidy after yourself and everything is going to work out great!

👉 Student Housing

These guidelines are meant for students who live in our student housing. Please read them and if you have any questions, please turn to the team! Student housing guidelines

👉 Study Space

  1. Monitor Identification:
    • Register your monitor with Ingrid or Annika to include a kood/Võru sticker on it, so that others won’t take it.
  1. Cleanliness:
    • Do not leave trash on your tables (empty plastic bottles, candy/snack wrappers & etc). Dispose waste appropriately.
    • No candles and perfume sprays are allowed!
    • You are allowed to eat at the armchair area and NOT your table. Small snacking is allowed. Please clean up after yourself right after.
  1. Mugs and Cups:
    • No dirty mugs, cups, or plates should be left unattended on tables. If left dirty or moldy, we reserve the right to dispose of them.
  1. Table Decoration:
    • Personalize and decorate only your assigned table. Any exceptions require prior approval from Ingrid.
  1. Table Usage:
    • For permanent tables, individuals are allowed to use ONE table at a time.
  1. Table Checks:
    • Regular table checks are conducted every 2 weeks. Non-compliance with guidelines, especially leaving a mess, may result in a notice and/or the school staff has a right to throw away the dirty items. Please follow those guidelines to maintain a clean and welcoming co-working space!

‼️The Don’ts ‼️

  • Don't damage or deface kood/Järva or its fixtures, décor or furnishings.
  • Don’t break web, servers, devices or passwords or do other mean things.
  • Don’t download or send anything that could break the Internet, or someone’s devices or property. Nor anything you wouldn’t want your mother to see.
  • Don’t steal stuff! Using someone else’s physical or intellectual property is considered stealing. And stealing is a punishable crime. We will act accordingly.
  • Don’t do drugs! The same goes with alcohol, especially within the school premises. It is strictly prohibited and police will be involved if needed.
  • Don’t smoke/vape indoors or on the school premises, this includes the terrace. It has already been restricted in Estonia since 2005.
  • Don’t act against the applicable laws, rules, and regulations of the Republic of Estonia.
  • Don’t cause or participate in any kind of fights or cause discomfort to anyone, neither mental nor physical.
  • Don’t create a false identity to confuse others.
