Common Areas

  • 1st floor is a Lounge Area and community space. You can also feel free to study there.
  • 2nd floor is Dormitory. There are 2 kitchens, showers and dormitory rooms for 5 people.
  • 3rd floor is the Silent Area, which is meant for intense studying and focused work. Please keep that in mind and do not talk loudly or make noise here. Also equipped with a kitchen corner + couches. During sprint it is only used for exams and hackathon style sleeping!
  • The 4th floor is the Data Center. 🚫We do not use or have access to this part of the building.
  • The 5th floor is the Co-working Area. It’s a great place for teamwork and catching up with your fellow students. Most of our events will take place there. Also equipped with a kitchen corner + couches.

Useful information

  • On the first day, you’ll receive your building access card. Only you can use it. If you lose it, please let the kood/Jõhvi team know as soon as possible!
    • You’ll have to return the access card at the end of the Selection Sprint - you can leave them at the security desk.
  • We are pet-friendly, but you have to let others know if you bring your pet. Some people have allergies and are not fans of dogs, cats or other pets. Please make a post about it a day before in Discord #general.
  • School is open 24/7, keep in mind that we have a hostel in the same building and if you wish to study at night, be respectful towards others.
  • The tap water is drinkable. ✅
  • Please wear slippers/indoor shoes in the building for your own comfort and tidiness!
  • Desks in the school building are hot desks. This means that you have to clean up after working behind a certain station! So that the next person can use it if they wish to do so.
  • Students can use both parking lots - the front and the one in the back of the building.

‼️The Don’ts ‼️

  • Don't damage or deface kood/Jõhvi or its fixtures, décor or furnishings.
  • Don’t break web, servers, devices or passwords or do other mean things.
  • Don’t download or send anything that could break the Internet, or someone’s devices or property. Nor anything you wouldn’t want your mother to see.
  • Don’t steal stuff! Using someone else’s physical or intellectual property is considered stealing. And stealing is a punishable crime. We will act accordingly.
  • Don’t smoke/vape indoors or on the school premises. It has already been restricted in Estonia since 2005.
  • No drinking alcohol on the school and dormitory premises!
  • Don’t act against the applicable laws, rules, and regulations of the Republic of Estonia.
  • Don’t cause or participate in any kind of fights or cause discomfort to anyone, neither mental nor physical.
  • Don’t create a false identity to confuse others.

And if something happens (e.g. coffee spilt on the couch, monitor broken, or something else) then let the team or the front desk know ASAP. Overall keep tidy after yourself and everything is going to work out great!
