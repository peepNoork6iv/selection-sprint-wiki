INTRODUCTION

The Code of Conduct (the Code) is a shared statement of our commitment to upholding the ethical, professional behavior and legal standards we use as the basis for our daily and long-term decisions and actions.
The Code applies to all members of the kood/Jõhvi community. For purposes of this Code, kood/Jõhvi community members are staff, students, including students of the Selection Sprint, volunteers, partners, visiting scholars and members of the board and council of kood/Jõhvi.

Members of kood/Jõhvi, collectively referred to this in Code as “community members”, are expected to act lawfully, honestly, and respectfully while studying or fulfilling any other duties connected to kood/Jõhvi. Community members are expected to uphold the excellent reputation of kood/Jõhvi. Students or any other community members who are unsure whether their conduct or the conduct of other kood/Jõhvi community members complies with this Code should contact school administration.

kood/Jõhvi shall ensure that the community has, at all times, the opportunity to examine the Code by storing it in a location accessible to the community members. kood/Jõhvi shall notify the community members of the amendments and supplements.

Community members are expected to act within the bounds of applicable laws, rules, and regulations of the Republic of Estonia. If you have any questions about the applicability or interpretation of any law, rule, or regulation in regard to your study or other duties connected to kood/Jõhvi, you should contact administration or Head of School. If any part of this Code conflicts with local laws or regulations, only the sections of this Code permitted by applicable laws and regulations will apply.

kood/Jõhvi has the right to record image, sound and video related to an individual or a group of individuals and upload them to kood/Jõhvi’s respectable social media platforms if the recordings were made on public grounds or events. An individual whose personal data has been used in a post, has the right to claim its removal at any time by contacting the kood/Jõhvi team.

Violations of the Code are taken very seriously and may lead to disciplinary action by kood/Jõhvi.

HARASSMENT

kood/Jõhvi does not tolerate unlawful harassment or any mistreatment by or of students, workers, guests, or partners on campus (in online either offline environment) or in a study-related situation based on sex, race, color, nationality, ethnic or national origin, ancestry, citizenship, religion (or belief, where applicable), age, physical or mental disability, medical condition, sexual orientation, or any other category protected under applicable local law. If you suspect harassment, discrimination, or retaliation has occurred, you are encouraged, and employees of kood/Jõhvi are required to promptly provide a written or oral complaint to the Head of School.

FORBIDDEN CONDUCT

It is unacceptable to engage in any unlawful, potentially harmful or dangerous activities on the premises of kood/Jõhvi or in a study-related situation. Community members must not violate any laws or rules of conduct regarding alcohol and/or drugs.

Forbidden conduct includes presence, possession, or consumption of alcohol; any distribution and/or sales of alcohol; conduct related to the use of alcohol; presence, possession, and use of drugs, controlled substances, or non-prescribed medication and behavior related to the use of drugs.

SAFETY

Each community member shall contribute to creating a safe environment, including complying with safety instructions and any rules establishing requirements for equipment possessed by them. Each community member must ensure, according to the instructions of kood/Jõhvi, that their activity is not harmful to their own or other persons’ lives or health and does not contaminate the environment. Community members must promptly notify the Head of School.

FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Community members are expected to act responsibly and exercise sound judgment with respect to matters involving finances, assets, intellectual or physical property, including but not limited to dormitories of kood/Jõhvi, including assets of partners and others related to the community members. Community members must treat the property of kood/Jõhvi as well as the property of other community members with care and return them in the same condition they were obtained. In case of any damages or any other material loss situations, everybody involved must cooperate with the Head of School to resolve the situation, including giving oral or written explanations. The Head of School will be in charge of delivering a fair solution based on the Code and local law, including covering losses (if necessary) by the person responsible for damages. Different aspects of covering losses and financial responsibilities can be more clearly defined in separate agreements, including rental agreements and any other types of agreements made between community members and business partners of kood/Jõhvi.

ACADEMIC ETHICS

Students have a responsibility to present work that is truly their own. Cheating and plagiarizing the work of others is a serious offense that can result in termination of the student contract by the kood/Jõhvi team.
All the (study)materials, including but not only limited to exams, quests, raids, and other intellectual property related to kood/Jõhvi or 01 Edu System, cannot be copied or shared with other community members or third parties.

UNDERAGE COMMUNITY MEMBERS

Community members who are under 18 years of age can partake in the academic and other activities at Selection Sprint with the explicit consent of a parent or guardian. kood/Jõhvi will enter into a separate agreement with the parent or guardian to help ensure the safety and lawful participation of underage community members. All community members must adhere to the Code regardless of age.
